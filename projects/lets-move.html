Let’s Move

social activism:

photography, print

Let’s Move! is a comprehensive initiative, launched by the First Lady, dedicated to solving the challenge of childhood obesity within a generation. Combining comprehensive strategies with common sense, Let’s Move! is about putting children on the path to a healthy future during their earliest months and years. Let’s Move! wanted a campaign to help promote healthier foods in schools and homes. This campaign ensured that every family has access to healthy, affordable food while getting kids to be more physically active. This campaign targets the millennial mothers by showing them what they wouldn’t let their children do.
