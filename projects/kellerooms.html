Kellerooms

web layout design:

photography, web

Kellerooms, Bed and Breakfast was renovated by Frank and Miriam Keller as a place to stay for visitors of the Humphrey, Nebraska area. Kellerooms has grown significantly but customers have begun to move towards other lodging in the last few years. Kellerooms would like to create a website design that highlights what their competitors are not: a local, home grown, and personable company. Kellerooms’ primary audience are couples in their mid 40’s to late 50’s with children. These couples are drawn to the websites focus on the “down home” nature of the bed and breakfast, Kellerooms’ reliability, the fact that it is family owned.
