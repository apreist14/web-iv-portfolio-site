Smoking Gun

package design:

photography, print

Smoking Gun Jerky, Inc. is a Beef Jerky Manufacturer founded in 2009 by entrepreneur Dave Ferguson in Lincoln, Nebraska. Smoking Gun was looking for a packaging system that better expressed their premium quality jerky that also highlighted their Nebraskan roots. This product line targets college–aged males who are willing to spend the money on a premium beef jerky.
